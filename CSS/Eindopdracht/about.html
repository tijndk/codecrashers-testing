about uomo

our story

UOMO Jewellery is essentially a jewellery brand, but we like to do things differently here. How? By doing everything boldly, creatively & passionately and never limiting ourselves! Driven by our ambition we embarked on this journey as a family-run jewellery brand in the summer of 2011, and to this day a touch of summer is part of the My Jewellery DNA all year round.

As sparkling connectors, we are committed to offering affordable jewellery design that is accessible and wearable for everyone and we are proud of the company we are today. Over the years the development of our signature UOMO brand has taught us many lessons, all of which have made us even more ambitious than ever before. By learning on every step of our journey, and being inspired by your needs and wishes, we have expanded our collections to include even more different and unique jewellery.

our mission

Our goal is to dress you up in the most effortless and attainable way, so you can sparkle throughout the daily celebration of your life and seal those precious moments together with us.

our vision

We believe you can accomplish anything with optimism and a powerful attitude, and are always looking for the moments that make life meaningful.

the company

UOMO Jewellery was founded in the summer of 2011, when our passion for jewellery led us to start a website followed by the first boutique a few years later. UOMO Jewellery rapidly grew online and established a thriving like-minded and fashion-forward community. We have since opened multiple boutiques which each represent that same quintessentially vibe throughout, so that when our customers set foot in our boutique they feel our passion and love for jewellery.

Our ever growing team of employees pour their love into UOMO Jewellery every day to make it a brand that represents the best of all of us, and of you. Join our community or visit us at one of our many boutiques in The Netherlands, United Kingdom, United States or Canada.
